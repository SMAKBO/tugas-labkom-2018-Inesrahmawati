What is the definiton of chemical property?

A chemical property is any of a material's properties that becomes evident during, or after, a chemical reaction; that is, any quality that can be established only by changing a subtance's chemical identity. Chemical properties cannot be determined just by viewing or touching the substance; the substance's internal structure must be affected to be investigated. When a subtance goes under a chemical reaction, the properties will changed drastically, resulting in chemical change.

What is the chemical reaction of Methoxyflurane?

Like the other ethers, the chemical reactions of Methoxyflurane include the reaction of oxidation, the cleavage by acid, reaction with PCl5, and the formation of Oxonium Salts.

The Preparatiion of Methoxyflurane

Methoxyflurane come as a functional group of ether. It can be made with react a propoxide compound with an alkylhalide.
